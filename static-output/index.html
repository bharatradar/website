Become a Feeder

Help expand coverage by setting up your own ADS-B receiver. All you need is a cheap RTL-SDR dongle and a Raspberry Pi.

1

Get Hardware

Raspberry Pi (any model) + RTL-SDR dongle (RTL2832U based). Total cost under ₹3,000.

2

Flash the Image

Download our pre-built image with readsb and mlat-client pre-configured. Just flash and boot.

3

Point to the Sky

Connect your antenna, connect to feed.bharatradar.com, and you're done.
